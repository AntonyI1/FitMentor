## Recommended Image Specifications

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Resolution**: 800x600 pixels minimum
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:3 or 16:9
- **File Size**: Keep under 500KB for fast loading
- **Quality**: Clear demonstration of proper form

## Image Sources

You can obtain exercise images from:
1. Stock photo websites (ensure proper licensing)
2. Custom photography with proper form demonstration
3. Open-source fitness image databases
4. Public domain exercise illustrations
